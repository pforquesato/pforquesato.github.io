Pedro Forquesato

FAPESP Researcher at University of São Paulo

I am FAPESP research fellow in the Department of Economics at the University of São Paulo.

Previously, I've been a researcher in iDados, post-doctoral fellow at the Catholic University of Rio de Janeiro (PUC-Rio), and visiting scholar at Harvard Kennedy School of Government. I have a PhD in Economics by PUC-Rio (2016).

I am interested in the research of actual and optimal economic policies, as well as the political determinants of the effectiveness of government in middle income countries. As hobbies, I like to play capoeira and literature. Also, I have a wonderful wife and a cute baby son.
